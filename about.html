About Us

Lazar Minkov was born in Sofia, Bulgaria. He moved to Germany when he was 12. In 2022 and eight schools later, he graduated a “Commercial School of Freising” in 2022. He moved in Summer 2022 to Bern, Switzerland. He started with his education in SAE Institute Zurich as Web design & Development. Apex Motorsport is the Project he created for his study.

He was so passionate about cars and motorsport, but till now he was searching for a way to show his knowledge and passion to the world. His Idea was to influence others to learn more about sports cars and their functionalities. From early he showed interest in loud and sporty cars. He built up knowledge for cars, by reading reviews taking a part in car communities, learning about specific models. He would dream of driving sports-car and building own “Project Car” as every one who has interest in car would.

The Name of the Website Apex Motorsport represents the top of the automotive world. That means the best sports cars made by well-known car companies to win in racing. The Main goal of the Project is to provide information and knowledge about sports cars. And build a strong car enthusiast community, after publishing the Website. Lazar is planing to include a page for motorcycles in the future. Showing the different types of motorcycles and how to choose a suitable motorcycle for your need.

Lazar Minkov started Apex Motorsport in 2023. And in 2024 finished the design of the Website.
